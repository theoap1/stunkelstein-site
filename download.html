How do you get started with Stunkelstein?

Download Stunkelstein free, open it, and create a game to get a 6-character code. Share the code or QR with your group, everyone joins on their own phone, and the app deals out secret roles — one of you will be the Stunk. From there you talk, bluff, and vote. No account, no sign-up, no setup.
